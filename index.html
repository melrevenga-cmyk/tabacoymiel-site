Our Story

Born from smoke
& sweetness.

Tabaco y Miel began in a Tulum kitchen — one wax pot, two attentive dogs, and a stubborn belief that a candle should smell like a place, not a perfume aisle.

Every candle is poured by hand in small batches. We use only the cleanest natural waxes, wooden wicks that crackle softly like a fire, and glass we recycle and rinse ourselves. No paraffin, no synthetic fragrance, no shortcuts.

It's a small operation — one maker, two dogs supervising from the floor with deep professional concern — and we like it that way.

i.The MakerPours every wick, by hand
ii.Chief InspectorThe chocolate one — quality control
iii.Head of MoraleThe golden one — studio greeter
